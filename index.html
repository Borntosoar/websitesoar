FAQ

Questions, answered.

When do drops release?+

Collections release in limited, numbered drops. Join the list for early access — once a drop sells out, it's gone.

How does sizing run?+

Pieces are cut for a considered, slightly relaxed fit. Each product lists measurements; when between sizes, size down for a cleaner line.

Shipping & returns?+

We ship worldwide from Canada. Unworn pieces can be returned within 30 days of delivery. Final-sale drops are marked at checkout.

Are pieces authenticated?+

Every garment is numbered and made in limited quantity from traceable, premium cloth.

Is there an age requirement?+

Yes — you must be at least 13 years old to use this site, and the age of majority in your region to purchase. See our Privacy Policy and Terms.
